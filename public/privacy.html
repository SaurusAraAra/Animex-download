Animex does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from your device. We do not require account registration, login credentials, or any form of personal identification to use the application.

Animex aggregates and displays anime content from third-party sources. We do not host or own any of the media streamed through the application. The availability, accuracy, and legality of such content is governed by the respective third-party providers.

These third-party sources may have their own data collection practices. We recommend reviewing their individual privacy policies.

04 Local Storage

Some preferences and watch history may be stored locally on your device to improve your experience. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's local storage through your device settings.

05 Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no centralized user database to secure or compromise. All streaming is performed directly from your device to the content sources.
